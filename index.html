Hi! I am Chien-Ting(建廷), a postdoctoral researcher in the astronomy and astrophysics department at the Penn State University. My primary research interests is realted to the nature of AGNs (the supermassive black holes) and their host galaxies. I use multiwavelength observations to study their evolution through cosmic time. In particular, I am heavily involved with the NuSTAR team. We utilize the unique high energy X-ray focusing space telescope to explore the supermassive black holes that could be hidden in the universe. My advisor is Professor Niel Brandt.
